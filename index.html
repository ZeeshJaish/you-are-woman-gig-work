The Mission

What is You Are Woman?

You Are Woman is a sacred gathering designed specifically for women seeking a profound touch from God. It is an intentional space created for healing, restoration, and the rediscovery of your God-given identity.

Whether you are in a season of waiting, a season of healing, or stepping into a new level of purpose, this conference is for you. We are gathering to break chains, renew minds, and empower you to walk confidently in who you were created to be.

This isn't just another event on the calendar. It's a transformative weekend marked by authentic community, powerful worship, and uncompromised truth.

Pastor Tabitha Taylor, founder of Kingdom of God Healing Center, stands as a testimony of the restoring power of Jesus Christ. She has known rejection. She has known insecurity. She has walked through seasons where identity felt buried under pain and misunderstanding.

But through the grace of God, Jesus renewed her mind, restored her worth, and rebuilt her from the inside out.

And Pastor Tabitha stands not as a personality, but as a witness:

Healing is real.
Freedom is real.
Identity in Christ can be recovered.

To God be the glory.
